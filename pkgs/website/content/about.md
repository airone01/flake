## The Homelab & Fleet

I maintain a multi-machine NixOS setup that runs my life and this website. If
you are curious, here is the current fleet:

- **`lyra`**: My main PC, running Hyprland and geared up for dev and
  ([some](https://dawn.wine/dawn-winery/dwproton/issues/3)) gaming. 6650xt, 64Gb
  DDR4, 12-threads Ryzen 5 5500 @ 3.6Ghz.
- **`cassiopeia`**: The trusty laptop. RTX 3060 (portable version), 16Gb DDR4,
  16-threads Ryzen 7 5800H @ 3.2Ghz.
- **`cetus`**: My local homelab server doing the heavy lifting. It's in my room,
  and prevents me from sleeping soundly, so I shut it down at night. 32Gb DDR3,
  16-threads Xeon E5-2665 @ 2.40GHz.
- **`hercules`**: The Oracle Cloud free VPS that keeps things accessible to the
  outside world. ARM64 4-threads, 24Gb RAM.

Across these machines, I self-host a bunch of things to keep my data
mine-including a Gitea forge, Searchix, and Vaultwarden.
