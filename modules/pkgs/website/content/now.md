## A.I.

I'm starting to understand why people hate AI so much, and see the consequences
of hardcore usage on my ability to write code. I now understand that AI does
make me write code faster, but is not worth it when you take into consideration
the time I spend rolling back changes and checking the quality of code I most of
the time don't even understand. I'm slowing it down a notch.
